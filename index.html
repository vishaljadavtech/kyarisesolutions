Why choose an independent expert instead of an agency?

You work directly with the person building your product — no middle layers, no delays. This means faster communication, quicker decisions, and a solution that aligns closely with your goals, all while keeping costs efficient.

What happens after the project is completed?

I provide ongoing support, maintenance, and feature enhancements as your business grows. Whether you need small updates or scaling support, you’ll have direct access for quick assistance whenever needed.

Can you work under NDA and formal agreements?

Yes, absolutely. I regularly work with NDAs and structured contracts to ensure complete confidentiality and protection of your intellectual property.

How do you ensure timely delivery?

Projects are broken down into clear milestones with defined timelines. You’ll receive regular updates, progress visibility, and consistent communication to ensure everything stays on track.

How do pricing and payments work?

I offer flexible pricing models — fixed cost for well-defined projects or hourly for ongoing work. Payments are milestone-based to ensure transparency and mutual trust.
